1. Installation & Installation Fees

Installation fees are non-refundable. The installation cost includes up to 150 feet of cable from the main road or main utility pole. Cable installation beyond 150 feet will attract an additional cost of $35.00 per foot (plus additional accessories used).

Important: On the day of installation, you must present the ID that is listed on your application form to the technician.

2. Equipment & Set Top Box

Each installation includes one set top box. This set top box remains the property of FYWY and is provided under a rental agreement with a monthly rental fee.

Customer Responsibility: Once equipment is placed on your premises, you are responsible for FYWY's property and will be held liable for any damage to our equipment.

3. Payment Terms

Payment is due on or before the 30th of each month (or at the end of the month in the case of February).

  • Late payments may result in service disconnection

4. Service Disconnection & Equipment Return

If service is disconnected:

  • The set top box must be returned to our office immediately
