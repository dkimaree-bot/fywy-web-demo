Equipment & Installation Policy

Installation

The installation cost includes up to 150 feet of cable from the main road or main utility pole. Cable installation beyond 150 feet attracts an additional cost of $35.00 per foot (plus accessories used).

Important: On the day of installation, you must present the ID listed on your application form to the technician.

Set Top Box

Each installation includes one set top box. This set top box remains the property of FYWY and is provided under a rental agreement with a monthly rental fee.

Additional Set Top Boxes

  • Purchase Option: $7,000 (one-time payment) + $300 monthly maintenance fee
  • Rental Option: Monthly plan cost + $1,000 additional monthly rental fee

Customer Responsibility

Once equipment is placed on your premises, you are responsible for FYWY’s property and may be held liable for damage, loss, or tampering.

Disconnection & Equipment Return

  • The set top box must be returned to our office immediately after disconnection.
  • If the box is not returned, the rental fee ($300/month) will continue to be charged.
  • If service is disconnected for six months, FYWY may remove equipment from your premises.
