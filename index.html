Your Local Tech Team

Our installation and support technicians are certified professionals who live right here in your community. They know the area, they know the infrastructure, and they know how to get you connected fast.

✓ Certified Installers ✓ Same-Day Service ✓ Local Knowledge ✓ Friendly Support

The Process

  1. You apply online or call us. We check if we can reach your address with our current setup.
  2. We schedule installation. Usually takes about 14 working days depending on demand and location.
  3. Technician comes out. Runs cable, sets up equipment, makes sure everything works before leaving.
  4. You're online. That's it. If something breaks later, we come fix it.
